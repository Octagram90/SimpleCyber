About

I am IT Security Professional with over 20 years’ experience in helping clients getting the most from their IT system.

Since 2014 I have focused on IT Security, gaining my OSCP and CISM, amongst other certifications, to help me to audit, pen test and assess client’s systems with a view to securing them as best as possible in line with their business risk appetite and available budget.

Cyber Essentials

This scheme backed by the NCSC, is a brilliant step in the right direction to secure your business by implementing simple, cost-effective controls. Our assessors, accedited by IASME can guide you through the process and, once at the required level, can certify you to Cyber Essentials.

Cyber Essentials PLUS

An addition to Cyber Essentials, CE Plus is an audited version of Cyber Essentials. It requires that we verify the 5 key controls that are part of Cyber Essentials are in place and working. It demonstrates your commitment to getting security right and is a requirement for securing some government contracts.
